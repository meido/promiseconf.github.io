Keynote: Dr. Forrest Shull

Dr. Forrest Shull is a division director at the Fraunhofer Center for Experimental Software Engineering in Maryland (CESE), a nonprofit research and tech transfer organization, where he leads the Measurement and Knowledge Management Division. At Fraunhofer CESE, he has been a lead researcher on projects for NASA's Office of Safety and Mission Assurance, the NASA Safety Center, the U.S. Department of Defense, the National Science Foundation, the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA), and companies such as Motorola and Fujitsu Labs of America. He is an adjunct professor at the University of Maryland College Park, and Editor-in-Chief of IEEE Software.
